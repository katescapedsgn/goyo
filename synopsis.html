Synopsis

A sequel to the critically and commercially successful 2015 film Heneral Luna, which chronicled Antonio Luna's life.

The story of Gregorio 'Goyo' del Pilar, one of the youngest Generals during the Philippine-American War who fought and died at the historic Battle of Tirad Pass.

A period drama film set during the Filipino-American war in the early 1900s), the revolution marches on against the Americans after the bloody death of General Antonio Luna. The conflicted philosophies behind the heroic struggle continue and become personified in the colorful character of General Gregorio "Goyo" del Pilar.

Genre: Action, Biography, Drama, History, War

Director: Jerrold Tarog

Writers: Jerrold Tarog, Rody Vera

Cast: Carlo Aquino, Arron Villaflor, Mon Confiado, Epy Quizon, Gwen Zamora, Alvin Anson, Empress Schuck, Rafa Siguion-Reyna
